What Is Deforestation?

Deforestation refers to the clearing or thinning of forests by human activity. It has significant ecological and societal impacts...

Why Is Deforestation a Problem?

  • Loss of biodiversity
  • Contribution to climate change
  • Disruption of local ecosystems and Indigenous communities

How We Can Help

  • Support reforestation efforts in Alberta and Canada
  • Volunteer or donate to local environmental groups
  • Reduce consumption of products linked to deforestation (e.g., palm oil, paper)
