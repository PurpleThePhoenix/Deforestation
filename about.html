Our Mission

We are a group of passionate students committed to raising awareness about deforestation. Our goal is to provide the public with the knowledge, resources, and actionable steps necessary to fight against deforestation.

Based in Alberta, Canada, we focus on the preservation of the province's boreal forests, which are under threat from logging, industrial development, and climate change.
